## Rationale

Across 32 ML/AI Platform Engineer postings observed between 2026-03-01 and 2026-06-01, the most material shift since the prior cycle is the GenAI platform mandate now landing at L30 ML Platform roles (not just at AI labs). Three previously-research-only surfaces are now explicit platform-team deliverables in postings at Reddit, Anthropic, DoorDash, Mixpanel, Glean, Fireworks, Together AI, OpenAI, Stripe, Red Hat, Samsara, Roblox, and Match Group: (1) LLM serving engines (vLLM, SGLang, TensorRT-LLM, KServe/LLM-D) integrated as platform-managed runtimes with autoscaling and capacity planning; (2) LLM Gateways with model routing, token/rate limits, intelligent failover, and prompt/version management as a platform feature; (3) Evaluation harnesses (including LLM-as-judge) and agent observability as platform-owned surfaces with SDK integration into the shipping process. Current Modules 01-09 and Projects 01-05 fully cover the classical ML platform surfaces (feature stores, registries, workflow orchestration, multi-tenancy, DX, SDKs) — postings still cite those, but they are no longer the differentiator. The three proposed modules and one project address the gaps that are now table stakes. Ray-on-Kubernetes is a smaller, exercise-level gap; everything else with thin evidence (RAG infra, research data platform, edge ML) stays on the watch list per the contract.
